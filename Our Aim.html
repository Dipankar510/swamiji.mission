Within two or three months our students must changed in their attitude, studies and above all in characters.OUR AIMS

2. Our aim is to provide a learning environment which values academic success, high moral standards, self-discipline and mutual respect for the cultures, opinions and values of others

3. Our aim is to focus on the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical

4. Our aim is to provide a learning environment in which all students are encouraged to actively participate and where students understand that making mistakes is an important part of the learning process

5.Our aim is to provide a curriculum and the support needed to enable our students to become confident, successful, independent learners

6. Our aim is to provide a rigorous curriculum which develops critical thinking skills, challenging each and every student to strive to be the best they can be

7. Our aim is to encourage students to set high goals, which can be broken down into a series of smaller achievable targets, and to motivate and inspire our students to work toward achieving their goal

8. Our aim is to provide support programmes to enable each and every student an equal opportunity to succeed in their studies

9. Our aim is to provide each child with an individual learning programme and a rich extracurricular programme to enable them to discover and develop their special talents and abilities

10. Our aim is to provide a pastoral care system which ensures that all students feel included and valued

11. Our aim is to provide a safe, comfortable learning environment where students feel secure, happy, and cared for
